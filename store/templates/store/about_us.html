About Somerset Shrimp Shack

Bringing Aquatic Beauty to Your Home

Our Story

Somerset Shrimp Shack began in 2024 with a simple passion: to share the stunning beauty of freshwater shrimp with fellow hobbyists across the UK. What started as a small personal collection quickly grew into something much bigger.

Founded by Emerzon Fitzgerald, our business is dedicated to providing healthy, vibrant shrimp and plants from ethically managed tanks. We believe that aquarium keeping should be sustainable, educational, and bring joy to hobbyists of all experience levels.

Our Mission

At Somerset Shrimp Shack, our mission is threefold:

  • Quality - We provide only the healthiest shrimp and plants, carefully selected and maintained in our state-of-the-art breeding facility.
  • Education - We share knowledge with our community through comprehensive care guides, responsive customer service, and regular workshops.
  • Sustainability - We're committed to ethical breeding practices that support conservation efforts and reduce impact on wild populations.

Our Facility

Located in the heart of Somerset, our facility is dedicated to the careful breeding and care of a wide range of freshwater shrimp and aquatic plants. Each tank is maintained to the highest standards, with daily monitoring of water parameters and the use of advanced filtration systems to ensure optimal living conditions.
