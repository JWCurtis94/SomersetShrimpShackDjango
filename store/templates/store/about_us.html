About Somerset Shrimp Shack

Bringing Aquatic Beauty to Your Home

Our Story

Somerset Shrimp Shack began in 2018 with a simple passion: to share the stunning beauty of freshwater shrimp with fellow hobbyists across the UK. What started as a small personal collection quickly grew into something much bigger.

Founded by marine biologist and aquarium enthusiast James Somerset, our business is dedicated to providing healthy, vibrant shrimp and plants from ethically managed breeding programs. We believe that aquarium keeping should be sustainable, educational, and bring joy to hobbyists of all experience levels.

Our Mission

At Somerset Shrimp Shack, our mission is threefold:

  • Quality - We provide only the healthiest shrimp and plants, carefully selected and maintained in our state-of-the-art breeding facility.
  • Education - We share knowledge with our community through comprehensive care guides, responsive customer service, and regular workshops.
  • Sustainability - We're committed to ethical breeding practices that support conservation efforts and reduce impact on wild populations.

Our Facility

Located in the heart of Somerset, our breeding facility features over 100 carefully maintained tanks dedicated to different shrimp varieties and aquatic plants. Our water parameters are monitored daily, and we use advanced filtration systems to ensure optimal conditions for all our livestock.

We continuously invest in research and development to improve our breeding techniques and develop new color varieties while maintaining genetic diversity and the health of our breeding colonies.
